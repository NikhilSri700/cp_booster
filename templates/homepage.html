Purpose

If you are into competitive programming then you must have heard about Codechef. And those who don't, Codechef is a competitive programming website aimed at providing a platform for students, young software professionals to practice, hone their programming skills through online contests. It organises 3 major contests every month, Codechef Long Challenge, Codechef Cook-off, and Codechef LunchTime.

Sometimes we want questions of all these contests at a single place sorted based on thier difficulty. And this is where one2sevenstars help you. You only need to choose contest name and the division.
